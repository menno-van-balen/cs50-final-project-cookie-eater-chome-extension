Whitelist Pages

Cookies are usefull, for websites where you login, so they'll remember you next time when you vissit the page. (Think Amazon, Prime, Netflixx, your library, newspaper etc.) To start you can select here some sites to your whitelist.

Tip: If you want to whitelist other domains click on the Cookie Eater icon under extensions when you are on the page you want to whitelist. Here you'll find two self-explanatory buttons. Go ahead try it right now!

Check the pages you want to whitelist

Delete Non Secure Cookies From Whitelist

Alternatively you could also only save the secure cookies from domains on your whitelist, the one that stores the important information, and delete the non secure cookies, which might be there for marketing purposes for instance.
Check the box if you want this

Auto Consent For Cookies

If you don't like pushing the button to allow for cookies everytime when you vissit a website, you can try this feature which does it for you.
It works in a lot of cases but sometimes not then you have to consent after all, if the site acts strange you'll have to pause Cookie Eater and refresh the page.
Check the box if you want this
